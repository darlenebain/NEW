Questions & Answers

Who is fbi?

fbi is a data/competitive intelligence platform trained to build and update company information from many sources across the internet. fbi is a new, one-stop due diligence platform that takes company searching to the next level by providing accessible, accurate and updated information on UK companies. Its quick and efficient search tool, combined with a vast and detailed business database will provide valuable assistance in searching all kinds of information related to any trading company. fbi provides its users with a complete detailed information package about any UK company, including contact information, recent news, company structures, financial information, payment history, directors and shareholders. Each business profile will additionally feature fbi's proprietary credit score and maximum credit limit, which is made up from a number of different factors to understand the financial position of a company and its level of financial risk.

Why should I register with fbi?

Finding information on companies in the UK is a time consuming and expensive task. A typical internet search can give you conflicting and inaccurate results. In addition, human research cannot keep up with today’s organizational changes and business developments. fbi scours the Internet continually, 24/7, monitoring and updating company data to provide users with the latest available information. fbi uses a variety of sources to curate this data. The raw data is collected, cross-checked for validity, and run through a rigorous series of algorithms to make sure the data we’re displaying is as accurate as possible. And all of this information is abolutely free.

Why should I use fbi?

Minimise the risk of bad debt, improve cash flow, offer the right amount of credit and take action against late payers. fbi has a wide range of purposes: private market intelligence, credit and business risk management, due diligence, market analysis, competitive analysis, business development, networking, and many other purposes.

What countries does fbi cover?

fbi currently provides data for companies based in the UK. We will shortly release other European jurisdictions in the relevant language of such jurisdiction.

Is access to the fbi platform really free?

Yes. You will need to register, but registration is free.

Why is turnover/profit not displayed for some companies?

Small companies in the UK with a turnover of £6.5 million are exempt from filing these figures – if they file them, the fbi platform will publish them. If a company has filed full accounts as opposed to abbreviated accounts, there is more data to capture.

What is a credit score and a credit limit?

Our credit score will take into consideration numerous factors including size and age of business, industry sectors, CCJ’s and bankruptcies. The fbi credit limit represents the maximum recommended credit exposure at any point in time.

Why do some companies have no credit limit?

It depends on a company's status and what type of accounts they’ve filed, i.e. non trading/dormant accounts. It could also indicate there is adverse information.

How is fbi different from other data providers?

fbi is more than a data provider - we are a technology-enabled platform that provides data, insights and intelligence to solve daily business challenges.
