BUSINESS INTELLIGENCE

Others do data, we do intelligence

Company Research

FBI helps businesses leverage analytics to derive meaningful insights from vast amounts of data in order to make better, smarter, real-time, fact-based decisions. Our analytics can identify innovative opportunities, and help to create new possibilities for a business and its customers. Businesses can help protect their reputation and minimise risk by ensuring they have accurate information on their customers, partners and suppliers.

Credit Risk Management

FBI is a powerful tool that helps companies identify potential risks quickly and provides you with a transparent view of your suppliers, clients and prospects helping you make better quality decisions. FBI combines extensive company and risk intelligence to help companies mitigate risk across portfolios, and calculates an indicative credit score and limit for each company in our database.

EASY TO USE DASHBOARDS

Our dashboard gets to the heart of risk quickly. It allows you to see a snapshot of your risk even when you have thousands of credits in a portfolio. It is particularly designed for private credit, the non-ISIN world where prices and data are not readily available.

DATA INTEGRATIONS

We plug into multiple sources of data, which is constantly evolving, to give clients the best picture of our risk. Our news feeds reflect contents of a clients’  portfolio; we use the latest AI thinking to filter out noise. We live in a world with almost too much data – our job is to use technology to filter it, distil and make it user friendly.

CLOUD BASED

We deliver a picture of your portfolio to you anywhere in the world on desktop, laptop or mobile. We allow your team to all see the same risk picture at the same time. Alerts can be sent directly to your phone so you can be on top of your risk at all times. News is sourced from hundreds of feeds reflecting what is in your portfolio. This isn’t just a configurable database on your desktop, it is so much more than that.
